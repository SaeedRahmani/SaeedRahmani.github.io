About Me

I am a PhD candidate, expected to graduate in January 2026, with extensive experience and interest in artificial intelligence for robotics. I have hands-on experience with Foundation Models (both LLMs and VLMs), World Models, and Reinforcement Learning for robot learning, especially in complex and uncertain environments.

My research focuses on developing intelligent systems for autonomous vehicles in mixed-autonomy scenarios, combining classical control methods like Model Predictive Control (MPC) with modern deep learning approaches. I have collaborated with leading companies including Toyota Motors Europe and BMW Group, and I'm currently conducting an exchange PhD at Oxford University's Department of Computer Science.
